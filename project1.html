Description

For this project, We had to make a game with two dice. We were separated by group, I was with Jérôme Sartiaux, Loïc Tuplink, Antoine Sprumont, Bastien Igot, Léandre Karasavas. The principle of the game was simple, we started with some points every then the player lunch the two dice, one player by one, the result of the two dice was sum. After we subtract the point by the result down to 0 the first player at 0 wins. But we put one obstacle if we go down 0, we don't subtract and stay like before we lunch.

The project was separet in two part : an electronique part and an It part, This two part were Connected by a communication card.

The electronique part had 14 leds, numbered from 1 to 7 by each part, each one represent the point of the dice like you see in the scematics, here is the logical for each led on :

  • 1 : D7
  • 2 : D5 D6
  • 3 : D7 D5 D6
  • 4 : D1 D2 D3 D4
  • 5 : D7 D1 D2 D3 D4
  • 6 : D5 D6 D3 D4 D1 D2

The electronique part had 4 different integrated circuit:

CD4093

The CD4093 are composed by 4 logic gate NAND, each gates switched at different points for positive and negative signals, the signal out was reversed (High or Low)

CD4069

The CD4069 his jobs was to reversed the polarisation of the signal
(High => Low or Low => High)

CD4029

The CD4029

Here was the logic of the programs :

When it was lunch, a loop makes a check to see if the points different to 0 if not, the listen to the communication card and take the 2 dice and does the sum after substract the points by the sum. it does an other verification, if the sum are bigger or equals to 0 if it was , the points are authorized if not the point stayed the same.
